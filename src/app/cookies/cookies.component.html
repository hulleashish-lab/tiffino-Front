Introduction

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform and to provide personalized services. This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies and other tracking technologies on the website and through our services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an online service. They help us remember your preferences, analyze usage patterns, and provide personalized content or ads.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function. They allow you to navigate the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the site.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website. They help us improve the functionality of our site, by analyzing data such as which pages are visited most frequently.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember your choices, such as language or region, and provide enhanced, more personal features.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver targeted ads based on your interests.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance your user experience.
  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Provide personalized content and ads.
  • Analyze site traffic and trends.
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

How to Control Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies for advertising and analytics purposes. These cookies are set by third parties, such as advertisers, and are used to track your online activity across different websites and services.

Cookie Consent
